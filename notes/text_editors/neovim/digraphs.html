Digraphs

Created at: 2025-01-12

Inserting a digraph

A digraph can be written when in INSERT mode by pressing CTRL+K followed by the sequence of characters that produce the digraph.

Examples:

Command: CTRL+K 2 2
Result:  ²

Command: CTRL+K a e
Result:  æ

Command: CTRL+K 1 2
Result:  ½

Displaying currently defined digraphs

Prefer dig! as it shows headers for better readability. Otherwise dig displays a more cluttered result.

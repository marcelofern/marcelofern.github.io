Where To Find Government Contracts

Created at: 2025-04-12

Government Electronic Tender Service (GETS)

This is a portal where most large government contracts are advertised.

It's free to use, but you have to register to view the details of listed opportunities. Registration requires information about the business.

Listings: https://www.gets.govt.nz/ExternalIndex.htm
